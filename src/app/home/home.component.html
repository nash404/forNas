A B O U T M E

Painting is my life and my great inspiration!!!!!

My name is Nataliia.I'm self - taught artist.
Once I’ve felt the desire to express my feelings, I began to paint pictures, I could not stop, my interest became more and more, I wanted to try different styles and techniques. I use different materials, trying to show and convey in my pictures all my feelings and emotions. I prefer to paint oil paintings, as oil gives a great sense of depth of color. In fact, everything depends on the specific state of the soul at the moment. Inspiration for me is the whole world that surrounds us reading and just Life.

To be honest, it's hard for me to write what inspired me to write this or that picture, I think my works speak for themselves, they contain my soul, feelings and emotions. I try to spend time working on pictures (more than on their descriptions), so they express everything I think about .... and they could give joy happiness to others.
